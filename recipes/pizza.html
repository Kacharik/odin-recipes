Pizza Mozarella

Description

Simple yet delicious, the combination of crispy pizza crust and melty-cheese-savory glory makes this mozzarella pizza sure to please everyone!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozarella
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Basil (optional)
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Chili flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Prepare your dough.
  3. Top the pizza
  4. Bake the pizza
  5. Serve
